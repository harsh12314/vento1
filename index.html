How It Works
01
Steam Exits

Precision channels give moisture a path out before it condenses on the lid and falls back as water onto your food.

02
Air Circulates

Continuous airflow keeps the interior below saturation — the threshold at which condensation, and sogginess, begins.

03
Structure Holds

Ventilation is built into the architecture — not punched in as holes — so the box stays rigid, spill-safe, and hygienic throughout delivery.
